About Me

This is the website of "realjame", AKA James, a programmer with some finished projects and a mountain of unfinished ones.

I'm primarily known for old Roblox stuff - making new games in decade-old versions of the Roblox client, or otherwise supporting such efforts. This is only the beginning. I also do general programming, currently only web development, but I am interested in other fields such as homebrew software.

The blog is the main part of this site. I probably left out a couple things I've written about, after all. Care to take a read?
